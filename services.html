Eligibility

Telehealth sessions are eligible for Medicare rebates or can be bulk billed.

You will need a referral letter and a Mental Health Care Plan (MHCP) from your GP to qualify for a Medicare rebate.

Work Cover Sessions

Approval from WorkCover is necessary for each client to access telehealth sessions. Please contact your WorkCover agent for assistance with this process.

Fee

Private clients are charged in accordance with the Medicare Benefits Schedule (MBS) for our services.
